F.A.Q

  • What's ECX?

    ECX is the Engineering Career eXpo, a student-run, non-profit body run under the University of Lagos Engineering Society (ULES).

  • What does ECX do?

    We prepare students for life after school and out in the industry by equipping them with technical and soft skills they will need to make the most of opportunities that come across their way.

  • What do you teach at ECX?

    We organize classes on technical skills (e.g. Frontend, Backend Web Development, Technical Writing, UI/UX, etc) and soft skills (such as presentation, negotiation and communication skills).

  • What is this “Grand Finale” about?

    The Grand Finale is an event marking the end of this session’s edition of activities, which will feature educative sessions on a wide range of topics, as well as prize-givings for our best students this session.

  • When do the classes hold?

    The classes for this session are over, but they ran twice a week for about 8 weeks, after which our students underwent tests and examinations to qualify them for the certification they receive at the end. Yes! We needed to make sure they learned something! Look out for the next edition! *wink*

  • What else do you do? Or you only host trainings?

    We had a lot of other fascinating activities in this edition! We had an online conference in June featuring top-notch speakers, a one-month long Internship in August, and many other things! And now we have a Grand Finale coming up soon that you MUST Register for!

  • How will I know when the next edition starts activities?

    Are you following us on our social media? Twitter, Instagram

  • What’s going to happen at the Grand Finale?

    Honest answer? A lot of exciting stuff! You might even be able to create a bot by the time we’re done! Register to find out more here
